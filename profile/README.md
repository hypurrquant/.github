# HypurrQuant

**A self-custodial DeFi terminal for the Hyperliquid ecosystem (HyperEVM).**

Provide liquidity (LP), swap, and trade perps in one place — compare vetted pools, see your **net APR before you deposit**, and open a position in **one click using multiple tokens**. Claim emissions and reinvest in a click, with Telegram out-of-range alerts. **Free to use, zero mint fees, fully non-custodial** — your funds never leave your wallet.
